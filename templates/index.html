⚙️ How It Works

1

You enter house details and select a city/area.

2

Flask backend receives data and passes it to the saved model.

3

Random Forest (100 decision trees) vote on the price.

4

The averaged prediction is returned as an estimated price.

🌳 Why Random Forest? It trains 100 different decision trees on random subsets of your data, then averages their predictions — reducing overfitting and typically achieving 15–30% better accuracy than a single Linear Regression model.
